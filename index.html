About Us

Our great-grandfather started this company in 1962 with the idea of using dense, Coloradan Quaking Aspen wood to produce cooler pads and other related products. Since then, we have grown to be a household name in the Valley, supplying our trademark Aspen cooler pads to businesses all over California. With over five decades worth of manufacturing experience, we can confidently guarantee that our product is the best on the market. Every Aspen wood product we make is handcrafted and inspected by highly experienced professionals to ensure maximum quality. We take the time to do things the right way.
That's the Johnston way.
